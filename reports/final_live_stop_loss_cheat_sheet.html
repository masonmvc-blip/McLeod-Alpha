Execution Rules

These are the live enforcement rules pulled from the current engine.

  • Stops ratchet upward only; they never move down.
  • At +1%, +2%, +3%, and +4%, the live exit reasons are 1% Stop, 2% Stop, 3% Stop, and 4% Stop.
  • Protective broker order uses SELL_TO_CLOSE STOP_LIMIT.
  • Stop checks use option bid first, then mark as fallback.
  • Maximum holding period is 20 minutes; close through the broker-safe exit path with reason MAX_HOLD_20_MIN.
  • If broker stop sync fails, the engine closes the position to avoid unprotected exposure.
Live contract: the bot will only increase protection as profit rises.
No down-ratcheting, no unprotected live positions.

- The early upside move combined a one-minute recovery, volume, and a higher-timeframe trend context. The source nevertheless noted that the market was extended and that near-term resistance and end-of-month profit-taking remained plausible.

- The day demonstrated both an upside continuation and a later retracement. The room repeatedly adjusted its view as price either held or failed around moving averages, rather than treating the early directional thesis as permanent.
- Option commentary highlighted the relevance of volume, spread, contract cost, and the mismatch between underlying movement and option execution. Source-side trade sizing and targets are not carried forward.

# McLeod Alpha Research Report: May 11, 2026 Trading Room

## Scope and Evidence

Source recording: Day Trade SPY, "Trading Room Video Recording - May 11, 2026" (1:10:25). The Vimeo caption transcript was reviewed from the authenticated recording page. This is external qualitative research, not a live trading instruction.

## Observations

- The session opened with a rapid advance through nearby resistance, followed by repeated new highs. The room treated the initial move as strong only after it moved through clustered moving averages, prior peaks, pivot resistance, and then held or retested those areas.
- The discussion distinguished an extended impulse from an immediately repeatable setup. After two strong opening legs, the room expected a deeper test, consolidation, or a flag/pennant rather than assuming uninterrupted continuation.
- One-minute and five-minute structure were used together: the faster view supplied local confirmation while the slower view framed the larger move and later consolidation.
- Volume was described as important for a renewed break. A visually constructive pattern without adequate volume was explicitly treated as vulnerable to a false move.
- Macro commentary centered on oil, inflation, Fed speakers, and upcoming inflation data, but the observed intraday discussion repeatedly returned to whether price held structure rather than treating macro narratives as timing signals.

## Research Implications

1. Test `OPENING_BREAK_CONFIRMED` only when a break clears local congestion and is followed by a close-side hold or successful retest.
2. Label the first two large opening legs as `EXTENDED_IMPULSE`; measure whether subsequent continuation is more reliable after a consolidation and volume return than during immediate re-entry attempts.
3. Record one-minute confirmation, five-minute structure, volume relative to the preceding consolidation, and distance to the next structural zone.
4. Isolate inflation, energy, and Fed-event context with `EVENT_WINDOW` without converting commentary into a directional feature.

## Decision

No live entry, exit, stop, sizing, or directional policy changes. The useful output is a research-only hypothesis that an opening advance becomes more durable after structural acceptance and a volume-supported consolidation, not merely after its first rapid extension.

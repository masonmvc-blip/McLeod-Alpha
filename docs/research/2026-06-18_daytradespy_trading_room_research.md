# McLeod Alpha Research Report: June 18, 2026 Trading Room

## Scope and Evidence

Source recording: Day Trade SPY, "Trading Room Video Recording - June 18, 2026" (1:34:41). The browser-visible Vimeo transcript was reviewed on July 22, 2026. This is external qualitative research, not a live trading instruction.

## Observations

- The room repeatedly returned to moving-average and intraday reference-level interaction rather than relying on close-location forecasts.
- Price targets and predicted closing levels were presented as conditional scenarios; the transcript does not establish them as executable system parameters.
- The extended session reinforces the need to distinguish discussion of a level from evidence that price accepted, rejected, or held that level.

## Research Implications

1. Persist the sequence of level test, close-through, retest, and hold for replay classification.
2. Compare target achievement with intervening friction and executable option bid/ask/mark data.
3. Keep presentation-level projections observational until validated out of sample.

## Decision

No live parameter changes. The session supports confirmation-first, structure-aware replay work only.

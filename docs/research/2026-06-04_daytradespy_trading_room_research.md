- The session emphasized low-volume pullbacks, higher lows, and volume-backed breaks as supportive details, while also acknowledging that a strong source-side directional view could be wrong.
- Macro and IPO commentary supplied context only; the actual intraday decisions centered on price acceptance, volume, and available room to the next level.

3. Measure whether low-volume pullbacks after a volume-backed break improve follow-through conditional on remaining structural room.

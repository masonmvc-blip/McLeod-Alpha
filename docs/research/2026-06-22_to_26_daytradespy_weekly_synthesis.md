## Research Decisions

1. Prioritize executable option bid, ask, mark, MFE, and MAE telemetry before evaluating source-style percentage targets.
2. Run a replay experiment comparing first-breakout entries with accepted-breakout entries, controlling for structural room and opening volume.
3. Label sharp opening selloffs separately and require support acceptance before testing reversal admission.
4. Treat price references and discretionary intraday targets in the source as observations, not deployable parameters.
5. Do not adopt averaging, reloading, directional bias, or any live rule change without replay and out-of-sample validation.

## Conclusion

The week supports a confirmation-first framework: let volume and acceptance validate the move, assess room through known structure, and avoid treating a quick extension or reversal as sufficient evidence by itself.

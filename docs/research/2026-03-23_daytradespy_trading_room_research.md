## Observations

- The discussion tracked successive resistance levels, a trend-line test, and an option premium effect that could offset delta exposure even as the underlying moved.
- Visual review, underlying bars, executable option bid/ask/mark data, and trade-ledger reconciliation remain unavailable; the transcript cannot validate a chart pattern, trade fill, or reported result.

3. Pair underlying movement with option delta, premium change, implied volatility, and spread in replay so directional correctness is not treated as an option outcome proxy.
